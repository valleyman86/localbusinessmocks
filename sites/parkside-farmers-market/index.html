Parkside Farmers Market

Fresh produce, quality herbs and spices, and Middle Eastern pantry staples in the Sunset District.

555 Taraval St, San Francisco, CA 94116

Why locals stop in

Parkside is known for quality vegetables, practical everyday groceries, and a deep bench of Middle Eastern ingredients that are hard to find elsewhere.

The shelves run from fresh produce and herbs to pita, spices, cheese, and imported pantry picks.

Hours

  • Mon-Sat: 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM
  • Sun: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Phone: (415) 681-5563

Visit Parkside

For produce runs, dinner ingredients, and specialty spice restocks, this is one of Taraval's reliable neighborhood stops.
